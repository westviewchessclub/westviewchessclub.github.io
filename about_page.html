About Us

Westview Chess Club was created to accomplish one goal, to bring together people who have a passion for chess. For a hour and a half, people have the opportunity to sit in a quiet room and play the beautiful game with friends or other members of the club. Our club is open-ended meaning that anyone can join at any time of the year. We hold our meetings every Thursday after school in Room South 127. The only thing you need to bring is yourself or a friend if you wish. The boards and pieces are provided by the club itself, as well as timers for those who like playing under the clock. If playing chess is one of your interests then come on by and see if you have what it takes to beat some of the best players in the school. See you Thursday!
